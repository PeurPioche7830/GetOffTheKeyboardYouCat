# Get Off The Keyboard, You Cat!
Basically the mac app "cats lock" for broke and macless people

Did your cat ever walk on your keyboard?
Did you ever wish you could stop it from using your computer for you without having to be here to get it off with your own hands?
Well don't worry, "Get off the keyboard, you cat!" is here to scare lock your computer and potentially scare away your feline without spending a dime on a paid, closed source, mac only app, mate!

Don't take this program too seriously, it's basically a coding exercise for me to learn python and tkinter without AI.

You need Python and Tkinter to get the .py running.
