# Get Off The Keyboard, You Cat!
Basically the mac app "cats lock" for broke and macless people

Did your cat ever walk on your keyboard?
Did somebody ever slam your keyboard while you were on an important window and made you send an embarrasing text to your mom?
Did you ever wish you could get rid of those problems without physical violence?
Don't worry, "Get off the keyboard, you cat!" is here to scare lock your computer and potentially scare away your feline without spending a dime, mate!

Don't take this program too seriously, it's basically a coding exercise for me to learn python and tkinter without AI.
